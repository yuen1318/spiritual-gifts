# Gifts of the Holy Spirit (Simple Explanation with Bible Verses and Examples)

## Introduction

A spiritual gift is a special ability given by the Holy Spirit to help others, build up the Church, and glorify God.

> “There are different kinds of spiritual gifts but the same Spirit…”  
> — 1 Corinthians 12:4–7

These gifts are not for personal glory but for serving others.

# Conclusion

The Holy Spirit gives different gifts to different people.

> “But one and the same Spirit produces all of these…”  
> — 1 Corinthians 12:11

The goal is not comparison.

The goal is service.

👉 The question is not “What gift do I want?”  
👉 The question is “How is God calling me to serve others?”
